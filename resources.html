Travel Resources

Airfare

Skyscanner

A wonderful tool for comparing flight prices and making sure you aren't tricked into something that's not the absolute lowest price. Great at checking sites other flight planners seem to skip. Personally what I use more often than not but feel free to check the other options for better prices.

Airfare Watchdog

I love Watchdog but it seems primarily for staying in the United States. Great prices and great tools to check but not a ton of overseas options. Not a deal breaker obviously if you're not looking for overseas.

Google Flights

Great for finding several different options. They seem to check most of the major outlets and do a good side by side comparison. I personally recommend just looking with this tool but booking direct. Not a fan of Google as a whole.
